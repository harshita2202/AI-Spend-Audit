## FAQ

**Q: Is this really free?**  
Yes. No credit card, no trial period. We make money if you choose 
to buy AI credits through Credex — but only if the savings are real 
and you decide it's worth it.

**Q: Do you store my data?**  
Your audit is saved anonymously. If you share your results link, 
only the tool names and savings numbers are visible — never your 
email or company name.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**  
Every recommendation is based on current published pricing from 
official vendor pages, verified weekly. The logic is transparent — 
each suggestion shows the exact math behind it.

**Q: What AI tools does it cover?**  
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, 
Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools added based on demand.

**Q: What is Credex?**  
Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits from companies 
that over-purchased. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll 
show you how Credex can help you get the same tools for less.
