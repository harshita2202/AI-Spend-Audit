# Unit economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal: a 10-person startup buying Cursor Business 
  ($40/seat × 10 = $400/mo retail) gets it at 30% discount 
  = saves $120/mo, pays Credex $280/mo
- Credex margin on arbitraged credits: ~20% = $56/mo gross margin
- Average contract length: 8 months (startup lifecycle)
- LTV per converted customer: $56 × 8 = **$448**

For enterprise deals (50+ seats): LTV = $2,000–$5,000

**Conservative blended LTV: $600 per converted customer**

## CAC by channel

| Channel | Cost | Conversions | CAC |
|---------|------|-------------|-----|
| Hacker News Show HN | $0 | ~8 customers | $0 |
| Twitter organic | $0 | ~3 customers | $0 |
| Newsletter placement | ~$200 (time cost) | ~5 customers | $40 |
| Cold outreach (time) | ~$150 (time cost) | ~3 customers | $50 |

**Blended CAC at launch: ~$15** (mostly time, not cash)

## Conversion funnel

| Stage | Rate | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Visitor → audit started | 30% | High intent traffic from HN/Twitter |
| Audit started → completed | 70% | Form is short, persists on reload |
| Completed → email captured | 35% | Value shown first, then gate |
| Email → Credex consultation | 8% | Only high-savings audits see CTA |
| Consultation → purchase | 25% | Credex's existing close rate |

**Overall: 1000 visitors → 300 audits → 210 completed → 74 emails 
→ 6 consultations → 1.5 purchases**

At $600 LTV and $15 CAC: **40× ROI on marketing spend**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR = ~1,667 active Credex customers paying $50/mo average

Assumptions:
- Tool drives 500 audits/month by month 6 (achievable with SEO 
  + word of mouth compounding)
- 35% email capture rate = 175 leads/month
- 8% → consultation = 14 consultations/month
- 25% close = 3.5 new customers/month
- Ramp to 500 audits/month takes 6 months
- Months 7–18 = 12 months × 3.5 customers/month = 42 customers/month 
  net new
- 42 × 12 × $600 LTV ≈ **$302k ARR from tool alone**

To hit $1M ARR: tool contributes ~$300k, remaining $700k requires 
Credex's direct sales motion running in parallel, plus repeat 
purchases and upsells from tool-sourced customers.

**What must be true:** The tool must reach 500+ audits/month. 
That requires either a viral coefficient > 1 (each user refers 1+ 
others via share URL) or sustained SEO traffic from long-tail 
"AI tool comparison" queries. The share URL is designed for the 
former; blog content strategy handles the latter.
